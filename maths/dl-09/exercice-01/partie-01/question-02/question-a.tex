Soit $x \in [0, 1]\\$
  Soit $h \in \left]-\frac{1}{2}, \frac{1}{2}\right[$
\[
  \begin{array}{crcl}
                & g(x)          & = & \sum\limits_{n=2}^{+\infty}{\frac{2x}{n^2-x^2}} \\\\
    \Rightarrow & g(x+h) - g(x) & = & 2\sum\limits_{n=2}^{+\infty}{\left(
    \frac{x+h}{n^2-(x+h)^2}
    -
    \frac{x}{n^2-x^2}
    \right)}                                                                          \\\\
  \end{array}
\]
