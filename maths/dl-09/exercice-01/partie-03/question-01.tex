$cotan(x) = \frac{cos(x)}{sin(x)}$\\
Soit $x\in\mathbb{R}$
\[
  \begin{array}{rl}
                & cotan(x)=y                         \\
    \Rightarrow & \frac{cos(x)}{sin(x)} = y          \\
    \Rightarrow & x = arctan\left(\frac{1}{y}\right) \\
  \end{array}
\]
\begin{result}
  $I = ]0, \pi[$
\end{result}
