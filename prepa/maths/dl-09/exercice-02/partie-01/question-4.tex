Par propriété :
\[
  \left\{
  \begin{array}{rcl}
    E(Y) & = & G_Y'(1)                        \\
    V(Y) & = & G_Y'(1) + G_Y''(1) - G_Y'(1)^2
  \end{array}
  \right.
\]
D'où :
\begin{result}
  $
    \left\{
      \begin{array}{rcl}
        E(Y) & = & \frac{1}{q}   \\\\
        V(Y) & = & \frac{p}{q^2}
      \end{array}
    \right.
  $
\end{result}
